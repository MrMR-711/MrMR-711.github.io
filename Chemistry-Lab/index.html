Chemistry Lab

A comprehensive simulation lab for exploring chemical elements

Android Version Web Version

Key Features

Explore the capabilities that Chemist Lab offers for your.

Atomic Model

Visualizes and designs atoms. Showing complete properties like conditions, standard atomic mass, etc. of the atom.

Modify Atoms

Feature to manually change neutrons, protons and electrons. Discover isotopes of atoms by changing the number of neutrons!

Search Atoms

Search for atoms by their chemical symbol, name, or atomic number!
